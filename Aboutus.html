About Us

Get To KnowWho We Are?

At Amani Security Services, we are a dynamic and forward-thinking company committed to delivering innovative and reliable security solutions. Operating under strict British Standards (BS7858 for staff vetting and BS7499 for security officers and mobile patrol services), we uphold the highest standards of integrity, professionalism, and excellence. Our team of qualified professionals is dedicated to the continuous growth and improvement of our services. We value our people as our greatest asset, offering fair recruitment practices, career development opportunities, and ongoing training under our Equal Opportunity policy.

WhatWe Do?

We provide a comprehensive range of Security Management Services, tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients. From highly trained security officers to mobile patrols, our personnel are prepared for traditional duties as well as additional responsibilities like reception tasks, stock control, administrative support, and dispute resolution. Through rigorous supervision and performance inspections, we ensure quality service delivery at every level. Our client reporting system and regular site review meetings with our Operations Manager allow us to maintain transparency and continuously improve our service effectiveness.

OurMission

Our mission at Amani Security Services is to provide trusted, high-quality security solutions that go beyond expectations. We are driven by a commitment to protect our clients’ people, assets, and reputation while creating a safe and secure environment through personalized service, professional excellence, and strong client partnerships. Your security is our priority—and our responsibility.
